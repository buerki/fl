There are a number of reasons why Italy is a good country to visit for a vacation. First, people who visit Italy can eat many kinds of delicious Italian foods. Italian food is healthy and delicious. Therefore, it registered Intangible cultural heritage, UNESCO in 2010. For example, pizza and spaghetti are popular foods in Italy. In addition, Italian food is unique. There are forty world cultural heritages and one world natural heritage. For example, Trevi Fountain and Basilica di San Pietro are one of the world cultural heritages. In addition, Trevi Fountain has three legends. If people throw coin backward once, they can come Italy again. If people throw backward coin twice, their love comes true. If people throw backward coin three times, they can finish their girlfriend’s or boyfriend’s relationship.
