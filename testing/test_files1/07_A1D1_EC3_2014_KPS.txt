Fiji is a good country for a vacation
There are a number of reasons why Fiji is a good country to visit for a vacation. First, there are a lot of beautiful nature. Especially, it is said that beach is very beautiful. For example, sea of Mana island. Mana Island is isolated island. It is said that this sea is the most famous and beautiful in Fiji. We can see the beautiful scenery.　Second, there are various foods which are different from Japanese food. In addition, they are very cheap and delicious. For example, Indian food, Chinese food, and various fruits. The fruits are mango, banana, pineapple…and more. We can enjoy eating various foods. Thus, if people go to Fiji for a vacation, they can relax very much and we can encounter different cultures.
