France is a Good Country for a Vacation
	There are a number of reasons why France is a good country to visit for a vacation. First, if people visit France, they can visit many famous buildings. There are built a long time ago. We can learn French history. For example, Eiffel tower, Arc de Triomphe, and Louvre Museum. There are popular in the world, so many tourist have visited France. Second, France has many wonderful foods. French is popular in Japan. Japanese people often eat French. For example, Pilaf, Gratin, and Bouillabaisse, and Meniere. A lot of Japanese like the taste of these dishes. Thus, if people go to France for a Vacation, they can see many famous buildings, and they can eat a lot of tasty food.
